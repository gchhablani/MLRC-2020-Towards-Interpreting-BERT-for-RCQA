For some decades prior to the First Palestine Intifada in 1987 , the Muslim Brotherhood in Palestine took a " quiescent " stance towards Israel , focusing on preaching , education and social services , and benefiting from Israel ' s " indulgence " to build up a network of mosques and charitable organizations .
